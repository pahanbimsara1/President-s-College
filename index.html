About Our School

President's college Minuwangoda is a respected school with a mission to provide quality education and shape future leaders. Established on February 9, 1979, our school promotes academic excellence, creativity, and character development.

Academics & Facilities

We offer a well-rounded curriculum in science, math, arts, and technology. Our facilities include smart classrooms, computer labs, a library, sports grounds, and creative studios to support student development.
