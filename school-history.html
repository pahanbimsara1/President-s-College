Our School History

Shaping Future Generations Since 1979

Our Journey Begins

Established on February 9, 1979, President's College Minuwangoda started with a vision of excellence. With a small group of passionate teachers and eager students, the school laid its foundation for future success.

Milestones Over the Years

  • 1980s: First batch of O/L students graduated.
  • 1990s: Expanded academic streams and sports facilities.
  • 2000s: Opened ICT and Science laboratories.
  • 2010s: National and Provincial competition winners emerged.
  • 2020s: Full digital classroom integration and new ICT building opened.

Today and Beyond

With a legacy of discipline and dedication, President's College Minuwangoda continues its journey towards nurturing leaders, innovators, and responsible citizens for Sri Lanka's future.
